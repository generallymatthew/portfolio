The Philosophy

I am a UX professional driven by a strict outcomes-over-outputs methodology. With over two decades of experience navigating agency environments, corporate fintech, and enterprise SaaS, I have learned that the highest-leverage design decisions are those tethered directly to product strategy and business health. Delightful user experiences are secondary to positive financial outcomes; my goal is to ensure they are one and the same.

Key Areas of Impact & Expertise
Simplifying Enterprise Complexity

A proven track record of taking highly technical, friction-heavy legacy platforms and transforming them into intuitive, self-explanatory workflows. I specialize in designing for "infrequent users": seasonal or annual stakeholders who require zero learning curve to execute high-stakes tasks flawlessly.

Responsible AI Integration & Governance

Deeply engaged in the strategic transition from manual software oversight to high-leverage agentic AI workflows. I balance this innovation with strict product stewardship, advocating for AI ethics, user-facing verification frameworks, and knowing exactly when to halt a feature to protect resource allocation and system integrity.

Connecting UX to the Bottom Line

Co-authored an internal primer on UX and Finances to train design teams on how their work directly impacts corporate fiscal targets. I bridge the gap between design execution and executive leadership priorities, proving UX value through support-ticket reduction, conversion lifts, and churn mitigation.

Design Culture Architecture

Experienced in auditing operational gaps and rebuilding collaborative frameworks for fully distributed, remote teams. I focus on restoring peer-to-peer feedback loops to keep remote design organizations highly aligned and cross-functionally integrated.
